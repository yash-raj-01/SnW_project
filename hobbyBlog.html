Welcome to My Journey!

Hi! I'm a student who loves traveling during breaks. This blog shares my experiences from 6 amazing places.

Places I Visited

Janakpur (Nepal)

2015,2017,2020,2024

Crossed into Nepal! Visited the stunning Janaki Temple. So colorful and peaceful. Loved the local culture.

Jaipur

2021

The Pink City! Explored Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, and ate dal baati churma. Loved the colorful markets.

Udaipur

2021

City of Lakes! Boat ride on Lake Pichola and watched sunset from City Palace. Felt like royalty!

Banaras

2023

City where every sunrise paints the Ganga in gold and the soul feels eternal peace. The city of ghats and gods, where faith flows as endlessly as the river itself.

Delhi

2025

Started my trip in Delhi! Visited India Gate, Qutub Minar, and tried street food at Chandni Chowk. So much history!

Rishikesh

2025

Peaceful vibes by the Ganges. Did river rafting and saw the evening aarti at Triveni Ghat. Best sunrise ever!

Mumbai

2025

Busy but beautiful! Walked along Marine Drive, ate vada pav, and saw the Gateway of India. City never sleeps!
